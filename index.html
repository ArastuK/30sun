30 Sundays / Product Manager Assignment

Reducing Customer Anxiety After Booking

Arastu April 2026
Problem: Customers book high-value international trips (₹1-3L) with 30 Sundays. After paying, many feel anxious and confused about what happens next. They contact support frequently, and ops teams respond manually, increasing support load and reducing customer confidence.
Part 1 of 3

What causes anxiety and where it peaks

Research approach

I went through the 30 Sundays funnel as a customer before writing any of this.

  • 1 Submitted a trip inquiry on the website for Vietnam, 2 adults, 5 nights
  • 2 Received 3 curated itineraries via WhatsApp within 60 seconds from the AI bot
  • 3 Got assigned a dedicated consultant, call scheduled within 15 minutes
  • 4 Had a full consultation call covering pricing, visa process, and payment structure
  • 5 Read 30+ customer reviews on Holidify, App Store, and Google Reviews

What I found

Pre-sale experience

Bot replies in seconds. Consultant calls in 15 minutes. Quick, responsive, personal.

Payment structure

100% of flights + 10% of landing costs upfront. That's ₹60,000-85,000 on day one for a typical trip.

Post-sale experience

Communication slows down after payment. Customers lose visibility into what's happening with their trip.

Existing app

30 Sundays has a post-booking app with itinerary, document vault, and chat. But adoption is very low (12 App Store ratings). The consultant didn't mention it.

The journey after payment

Day 0: Quote received
Trust and excitement are high
Consultant sends a quote over WhatsApp. The couple discusses, compares options. Consultant is responsive, answers quickly.
Day 1-2: Payment
₹60,000+ to a company they found on Instagram
Payment link arrives. Card details go in. Doubts surface: cancellation policy, visa risk, whether this is a good deal. These are people used to ₹500 Swiggy orders. ₹60K+ to a company they've known for 48 hours is a completely different decision.
Day 3-10: The Void
Money is gone. No updates.
The consultant who called within 15 minutes pre-sale is now booking hotels and flights behind the scenes. The customer sees none of it. No confirmations, no timeline, no updates.
"Before payment, they are extremely sweet and constantly follow up. But once the payment is done, their response becomes very poor."Bharati, Mumbai · Holidify
Day 10-20: Visa processing
The thing that can cancel the entire trip
Documents get submitted, then silence. The customer doesn't know if the visa has been submitted, is processing, or is stuck. If it's rejected, they lose the trip and potentially the money.
Day 20-28: Pre-departure
Everything is scattered
Trip is days away. Vouchers, driver details, and transfer info are buried across 3 weeks of WhatsApp messages. No single place to find it all.
Day 29: Departure
Is someone picking us up?
At the airport with no driver name, no phone number, no transfer confirmation.
"No ground support, no planning."Vijay Pal, Pune · Holidify

Core insight

The pre-sale experience is fast, proactive, and personal. After payment, it goes quiet. The team is still working, but the customer can't see any of it.

Every "any update?" message is a customer asking: "Is anyone working on my trip?"
