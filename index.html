Reducing Post-Booking Anxiety at 30 Sundays

Arastu · Apr 2026
Problem: Customers book high-value international trips (₹1-3L) with 30 Sundays. After paying, many feel anxious about next steps. They contact support frequently, ops responds manually. Goal: reduce this anxiety through product.
1. Anxiety Map 2. Solution 3. Metrics

1. What causes anxiety and where it peaks

How I researched this

  1. Went through the 30 Sundays funnel as a customer (Vietnam, 2 adults, 5 nights)
  2. Received 3 itineraries via WhatsApp within 60 seconds from the AI bot
  3. Got assigned a consultant, call within 15 minutes, had full consultation
  4. Read 30+ reviews on Holidify (3.3★), App Store (4.0★), Google (4.6★)

Findings

AreaWhat I found
Pre-saleBot replies in seconds, consultant calls in 15 min. Fast, responsive, personal.
Payment100% of flights + 10% landing costs upfront = ₹60-85K on day one.
Post-saleCommunication slows down. Customers lose visibility into their trip status.
AppPost-booking app exists but barely adopted (12 App Store ratings). Consultant didn't mention it.

Anxiety peaks

WhenWhat happensLevel
Day 1-2Payment. ₹60K+ to a company found on Instagram 48 hours ago.High
Day 3-10The Void. Consultant booking behind the scenes. Customer sees nothing.Peak
Day 10-20Visa. Documents submitted, then silence. Rejection means losing trip + money.Peak
Day 20-28Pre-departure. Info scattered across weeks of WhatsApp messages.High
Core insight: Pre-sale is fast, proactive, personal. Post-payment goes quiet. The team is still working, but the customer can't see it. Every "any update?" message = "Is anyone working on my trip?"
